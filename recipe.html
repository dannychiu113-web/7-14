Homestyle Fried Rice

A quick, savory skillet meal that turns leftovers into something special.

Backstory and Creator

This fried rice recipe comes from a family tradition of using simple ingredients to make a filling meal. It was created by my grandmother, who believed every good dish should be flavorful, fast, and easy to share.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice, chilled
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp oil

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan and scramble the eggs.
  2. Add garlic and mixed vegetables, then stir-fry until tender.
  3. Mix in the chilled rice and break up any clumps.
  4. Pour in soy sauce and toss everything together evenly.
  5. Finish with green onions and serve hot.

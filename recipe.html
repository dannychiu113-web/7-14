Fried Rice

Backstory

This recipe comes from a really great friend I know.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups overnight rice
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 green onions, minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • oil

Preparation Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and scramble the eggs.
  2. Add garlic and mixed vegetables, then stir-fry.
  3. Mix in the chilled rice and break up clumps.
  4. Pour in soy sauce and mix well.
  5. Throw in green onions to finish.
